LocGuessr is a fun application that combines the power of¬†GPT-4 Vision, a sophisticated AI, with image analysis to guess the location a given picture was taken. This is a simple proof of concept where GPT-4 Vision will get smarter and start to recognize different cities and specific roads.

How It Works?

LocGuessr uses GPT-4 Vision to analyze your uploaded images and tries to determine where in the world the picture was possibly taken. The AI analyzes the image and makes an educated guess about the location. Currently the AI is able to get the country right 95% of the time, but the AI is unable to tell the states correctly 70% of the time.

Limits

The current implementation includes a daily limit of 50 image searches per day, throughout all users on the internet. This is to prevent me from going bankrupt üí∏. If you hit the limit, don't worry! It resets every day.
